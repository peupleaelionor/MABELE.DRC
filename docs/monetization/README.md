# MABELE — Monetization Strategy

## Principle

**Free for citizens. Paid for businesses.**

MABELE is built on the principle that digital access for ordinary Congolese citizens
must remain free and barrier-free. Revenue is generated exclusively from the business
layer — fairly, gradually, and in proportion to value delivered.

---

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Business Subscriptions (SaaS)

Monthly or yearly plans for merchants, agencies, and enterprises:

| Plan       | USD/mo | Primary Target |
|-----------|--------|---------------|
| Starter   | $9.99  | Freelancers, small sellers |
| Business  | $29.99 | SMEs, agencies, recruiters |
| Enterprise| $99.99 | Banks, institutions, large merchants |

Annual discount: ~25% off monthly rate.

### 2. Listing Boosts

Pay-per-boost to appear at the top of search results:
- 7-day boost: from $2
- 14-day boost: from $4
- 30-day boost: from $8

Available to all users (even free tier), paid à la carte.

### 3. Featured Placement

Homepage and category-page premium placement for businesses:
- Module banner: from $20/week
- Homepage carousel slot: from $50/week

### 4. Verification Services

- Business verification badge: $15 (one-time)
- ID/KYC verification: free for citizens, included in business plans
- Premium verification fast-track: $5

### 5. Merchant Tools (Business+)

- Merchant dashboard with analytics
- Bulk listing upload
- Inventory management
- Custom payment links
- QR code generation for physical stores

### 6. API Access (Business+)

- REST API for third-party integrations
- 1,000 req/day included in Business plan
- Additional usage: $0.001/request
- Enterprise: unlimited with SLA

### 7. Team Accounts (Business+)

- Up to 5 team members in Business plan
- $5/additional seat/month

### 8. Invoicing & Payment Tools (NKISI)

- Basic invoicing: free for all
- Advanced templates, recurring invoices, payment tracking: Business+
- SMS invoice delivery: $0.05/SMS

### 9. Optional Transaction Fees

Applied selectively where MABELE facilitates the payment:
- Marketplace transactions: 1.5% (capped at $5)
- Escrow payments: 2%
- Merchant QR collections: 1% (waived for Business+ plans)
- P2P transfers via KangaPay: free (wallet-to-wallet)

### 10. KangaPay Business

- Merchant collection accounts
- Float/float interest (future)
- Tontine management fee: 1% of pot (future)
- Savings product fee: 0.5% AUM (future)

---

## What Is Always Free for Citizens

- Search and browse all listings
- Post up to 5 listings/month
- Basic messaging
- Phone OTP authentication
- NKISI basic invoicing
- KangaPay wallet (receive, send, P2P)
- Tontine participation
- Agri marketplace browsing
- Congo Data public indicators

---

## Pricing Philosophy

- Prices calibrated to local DRC purchasing power
- Never block basic utility for ordinary users
- Earn trust before extracting value
- Scale revenue with ecosystem value, not extraction
- Transparent pricing — no hidden fees
- Mobile money-first payment for plans (no card required)
